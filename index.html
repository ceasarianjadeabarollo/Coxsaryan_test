    Welcome to my creative showcase! I am currently honing my craft as a trainee video editor for SILAKBO, a dynamic organization based at the South East Asian Institute of Technology (SEAIT) here in Tupi. While I bring a solid foundation of two years of video editing experience to the table, stepping into this trainee role has been an incredible period of growth and hands-on learning. For me, editing is about much more than just cutting and splicing clips together; it’s about capturing the authentic energy, passion, and heartbeat of our student community. As a trainee, every project I take on is a new opportunity to refine my technical skills and deepen my understanding of visual storytelling. Whether I am adjusting the pacing to build excitement, fine-tuning the colors to set the perfect mood, or syncing audio to elevate the narrative, my goal is always to translate raw footage into polished media that truly represents what our organization stands for. It's a challenging but incredibly rewarding process to help amplify our community's voices and document our history.
